About Us

Pure Esports is a brand new, deluxe esports center dedicated to bringing a fun and innovative experience that will redefine the way you game. With over 50+ high-tech computers and consoles, Pure Esports brings Gilbert, Arizona, an event packed esports stage and a stimulating social gaming environment to the local community. We cater to all ages, providing a safe and secure atmosphere for both the newcomer and experienced gamer.

We have the latest technology, featuring 2nd gen Ryzen cores and NVIDIA GTX 1070 gaming computers and Microsoft’s newest XBOX One X consoles. Play on our 144hz high-end monitors, XL racing chairs, and experience a jaw dropping space-age look that will leave you craving more. Our state-of-the-art server and enterprise fiber network allows for the fastest and best competitive esports venue Arizona has to offer.

Whether you are a casual gamer, seeking a gaming hub for you and your squad, or the competitive professional, Pure Esports is the gaming arena for you.
